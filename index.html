Our Mission

Our mission is to spread the teachings of the Quran and increase the understanding of the Quran among the people of Bangladesh. We believe that access to the holy Quran should be available to everyone, regardless of their economic circumstances. Through our free distribution program, we aim to reach schools, madrassas, and individual students across the nation, providing them with quality copies of the Quran in Bangla translation.
